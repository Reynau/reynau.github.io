Paint War

Paint War is an online multiplayer browser game where your objective is to paint more tiles than the enemy team. Each time your player walks over a tile, it paints to your team color. If you walk 4 times over the same tile without being painted meanwhile by another team, the tile gets blocked for your team and cannot be stolen anymore. If you walk over a tile from another team, it paints as it were the first time you walk over it. If you create a closed figure of blocked tiles, all the non-blocked tiles inside the figure will be fixed of your team color.

Do you want to try it? Play it on Heroku!
